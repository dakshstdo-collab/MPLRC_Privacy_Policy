Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 24, 2025

Diversion Calc ("App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. The App operates entirely offline and uses only local data for calculations.

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifiable information.
  • Local Inputs: User inputs such as area measurements (e.g., square feet, hectares) and selections (e.g., tehsil, village, diversion type) are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any servers.
  • Local Database: The App uses a pre-loaded database (villages.db) stored in the app's assets for village and local body information. This data is static and does not include user-specific information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since no personal information is collected, we do not use any user data for any purpose. All calculations are performed locally to provide estimates for land diversion rates based on government guidelines for Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

  • Local Processing: Inputs are used solely for on-device calculations and are not stored or shared.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: The App does not use analytics services, cookies, or tracking mechanisms.

3. Information We Share

We do not share any user information with third parties. As no data is collected, there is nothing to share.

  • No Data Sharing: No user data is shared with advertisers, partners, or any external entities.
  • Local Only: All operations are confined to the user's device.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited local data processed by the App.

  • Local Storage: User inputs are not saved persistently and are cleared upon app closure or reset.
  • Offline Operation: No data is transmitted over networks, eliminating risks associated with data transmission.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted here with a revised "Effective Date." Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
